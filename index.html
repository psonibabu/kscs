About Us

Kashi Sahu College has a vision of an education that would enable the youth to take part in the polity of the state & nation and to grow intellectually while at the same time retaining their rootedness in the cultural and civilizational values that India stands for.

Our Motto is to empower the quality education and excel in the field of research of global standard

To remain rooted in the proven, core traditions of the College, especially its spiritual foundation, in a secularizing and modern world.

To foster a deep sense of commitment in the fresh faculty appointees who hail from different traditions.
